# RenalPaw Therapeutics LinkedIn Company Page

Use this file to create the official LinkedIn company page for KSGC.

## Official Creation Path

1. Log in to your personal LinkedIn account on desktop.
2. Click **For Business** in the top-right corner.
3. Scroll to the bottom of the menu.
4. Click **Create a Company Page**.
5. Select **Company**.
6. Fill in the company profile fields below.
7. Create the page and copy the final company page URL back into the KSGC application.

If the creation button is unavailable, LinkedIn may require your personal account to have more than one connection. Add two or three real LinkedIn contacts first, then try again.

## Company Description

RenalPaw Therapeutics is a Beijing-based veterinary biotechnology startup developing disease-modifying therapeutics for feline chronic kidney disease. We combine feline renal biology, multi-omics data and AI-enabled target discovery to identify therapeutic targets and develop treatments designed to slow kidney function decline.

Our lead program, RX-01, focuses on mechanisms associated with renal inflammation, fibrosis and tubular injury. We aim to build a partner-ready veterinary therapeutic program through feline-specific validation, preclinical development and collaboration with animal-health partners in Korea and across Asia.
