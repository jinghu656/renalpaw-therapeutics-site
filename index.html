The Unmet Need

Cats need more than supportive care.

Feline chronic kidney disease is common, progressive and emotionally costly for families. Current care is dominated by diet, hydration and symptom management, leaving a clear opportunity for safe treatments that alter disease biology.

01

Progressive biology

Renal inflammation, fibrosis and tubular damage gradually reduce kidney function.

02

Long treatment window

Owners and veterinarians need chronic, tolerable therapies suitable for aging cats.

03

Commercial white space

Companion-animal pharma is seeking differentiated chronic disease pipelines.
